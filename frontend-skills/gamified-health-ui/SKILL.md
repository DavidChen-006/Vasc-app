# Gamified Health UI

## Purpose

Turn safe cardiovascular care behaviors into a motivating daily loop without creating shame, unsafe intensity pressure, or public comparison.

## Core Loop

1. Open app.
2. See cardio companion and today's Heart/Cardio Score.
3. Complete one safe mission.
4. Earn Pulse Points/XP.
5. Maintain or recover streak.
6. Close progress rings.
7. Unlock badges or rank progress.
8. See progress history and return tomorrow.

## Mechanics to Prefer

- Tiny daily missions.
- Compassionate streaks with freezes and medical pauses.
- Progress rings for categories like movement, monitoring, medication, learning, and recovery.
- Calendar heatmaps for consistency.
- Badges for safe adherence milestones.
- Weekly recaps focused on progress, not perfection.
- Personal bests against the user's own baseline.

## Health-Safe Reward Rules

- Reward consistency more than intensity.
- Reward care-plan adherence, check-ins, and learning.
- Use rest days as valid progress when appropriate.
- Make comeback flows easy after missed days.
- Prefer private progress by default.

## Avoid

- Public leaderboards in v1.
- Ranking patients by raw step count, speed, weight loss, blood pressure, or exertion.
- Punitive point loss for missed tasks.
- Notification spam or shame-based copy.
- Mechanics that make users push through concerning symptoms.
